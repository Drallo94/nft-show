Charged Punks – Stakable NFTs

ABOUT Charged Punks – Stakable NFTs

Battle Ape Game is the first in-browser, ready-to-play, NFT-based runner game on ETH. The gameplay is inspired by some of the classic runner/platform games like Super Mario and Sonic, revived with a Play-to-Earn model based on daily leaderboards.

Owning a Battle Ape NFT lets you play the Game and earn daily $KONGZ. Our vision is to create the ultimate community around a P2E game with engaging PvP and PvE mechanics. Use $KONGZ in the Marketplace to mint weapons, mutate your Apes, supercharge your runs and multiply your rewards.

In Battle Ape Game, your greatest weapon is your skill, not luck.
